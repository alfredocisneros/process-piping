About

Process Piping, Inc. has been in business since December 2001. We specialize in industrial and commercial piping services: Our goal is to provide exceptional service and to maintain long lasting relationships with our clients. We work on a range of systems from process piping, stainless sanitary food piping and tubing, steam systems, compressed air systems, hydraulics, pneumatics with a wide variety of piping materials such as hasteloy C-276, Teflon lined, fiberglass, stainless, carbon steel and plastics. We work in environments from food production plants, pharmaceutical plants, to foundries with the ability to meet your project needs and budget. We offer competitive pricing and an excellent safety record and program.

Dennis Ahler

He started business in December 2001 as an apprentice over thirty years ago.

The Experience

After working for several people he decided to go out on his own. He has over 30 years of experience in piping installations and fabrication.

Our Services

Process Piping, Inc. specializes in the installation of air, fuel, hydraulic, steam, fiberglass and stainless sanitary piping systems. We also do plumbing work from minor service repairs to waste treatment systems to inspection/removal of backflows as well as yearly inspection of backflows.
